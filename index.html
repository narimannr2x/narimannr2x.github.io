About

A clinical bridge for practical medical AI

I am a physician researcher based at the Gastroenterology and Liver Research Center, Taleghani Hospital, working on AI in gastroenterology and broader medical AI. My long-term direction is radiology AI and medical computer vision, but my broader interest is building useful systems that connect clinical reality with technical possibility.

My work sits between clinicians, researchers, engineers, and healthcare systems. I am strongest when a problem needs clinical context, a research frame, technical implementation, and a practical sense of how the result could be used or fail in real workflows.
